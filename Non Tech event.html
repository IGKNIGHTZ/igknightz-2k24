Non Technical Events

  • Story Naration

    Story narration is only for solo performers . Each story should be of maximum 3 minutes. Stories can be original, adapted, or authentic. Stories should be educational and not touch on sensitive topics like race, religion, stereotyping, or politics. Judges will give points based on the story and will announce the winner.

  • Art Out Of Waste

    Students can participate solo or in a team of two members. Waste materials should be brought by the participants. Waste material could be anything like tetra packs, bottles, newspapers, old utensils, jute material or any second hand items that otherwise would be thrown away. All projects should be safe to handle and socially and culturally appropriate. Participants will be judged on their artistic composition, eco-friendly rating, and creativity and design.

  • Debate

    Students can participate in a team of 4 members. Topic will be announced before the competition. Each speaker will have specific time time limit for speaking. Speakers should not use mixed languages. Speakers should also not insult, offend, or disrespect other debaters or judges. They should also not speak out of turn, interrupt an opponent, or sway, shuffle, or pace. Winners will be announced based on the points and performance.

  • Photography

    The photo must be in its original state and cannot be altered in any way. The content of a photograph must not be altered by adding, rearranging, reversing, distorting or removing people and/or objects from within the frame. Winners will be announced based on the numbers of likes the photograph gets in Instagram page.

  • Connection

    Participants will have to figure out the names of the movies or songs by connecting the photos displayed. Participants should give answer within the time limit. Participants giving more numbers of correct answers will be announced as winner.
