Technical Events

  • Product Development

    Participants can participate in a team of two or solo. Participants have to brainstorm the solution for the given technical problems within the given timeframe. The solution should include both the design and statement for the problem. Winners will be announced based on the points gained.

  • Group Discussion

    Participants has to participate individually. Topic for the GD will be given at the time of competition. Timeframe will be given for each speaker. Winners will be announced based on points given by the judges.

  • Paper Presentation

    The Paper Presentation Competition aims to accentuate analysis-based research work in students, develop their ability to bring out their ingenious selves, and gain expertise on the topics to enhance their knowledge and learning. The topics that the papers can be chosen by your own. It is advisable that the presentation focuses on one particular topic. Paper should not exceed 15 pages.

  • Coding and Decoding

    Participants should participate in a team of two or solo. In this participants have to decode the manufacturing processes involved in the displayed finished product within the given timeframe.

  • Cad Modeling

    Only for solo performers. Participants have to draft 3D model for the given 2D diagram. Participants should use solidworks software for designing. Timeframe for completing the design is 45 minutes. Winners will be announced based on the accuracy of the model.Register participants should bring your own laptop.
